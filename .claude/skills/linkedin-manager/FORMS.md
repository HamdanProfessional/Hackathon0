## Hashtag Strategy

### Branded Hashtags
- `#[YourCompany]` - Company name
- `#[YourCampaign]` - Campaign-specific

### Industry Hashtags
- `#[YourIndustry]` - Main industry
- `#[Niche]` - Specific niche
- `#[Topic]` - Relevant topics

### Engagement Hashtags
- `#Innovation`
- `#Technology`
- `#Business`
- `#Leadership`
- `#Growth`

### Hashtag Best Practices
- Use 3-5 hashtags per post
- Mix specific and broad tags
- Create branded hashtags for campaigns
- Research trending hashtags in your industry
- Avoid overused or irrelevant tags

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Posting Schedule

**Monday**: Industry insights or thought leadership
**Tuesday**: How-to or educational content
**Wednesday**: Case study or success story
**Thursday**: Behind-the-scenes or team content
**Friday**: Professional tips or weekend reading

### Optimal Posting Times

- **Best**: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM on weekdays
- **Good**: 7-8 AM, 1-3 PM on weekdays
- **Avoid**: Weekends (unless B2B with weekend audience)
- **Timezone**: Adjust to your target audience's timezone
