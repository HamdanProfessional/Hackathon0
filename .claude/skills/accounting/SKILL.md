# Accounting Manager

Manage, analyze, and report on business financial data including transactions, invoices, expenses, and revenue tracking.

## Purpose

The Accounting Manager skill **monitors** financial activity across the business, **categorizes** transactions, **tracks** invoices through their lifecycle, **analyzes** spending patterns, and **generates** financial reports for CEO briefings. This skill **ensures** accurate financial records while **providing** actionable insights for business decisions.

## Design Philosophy

- **Accurate First**: Every transaction recorded and categorized correctly
- **Proactive Monitoring**: Flag unusual expenses and overdue invoices automatically
- **Audit Trail**: Complete history of all financial activity
- **Integrated**: Works with CEO Briefing for financial insights
- **Approval-Required**: All actions require human approval before execution

## Workflow

1. **Monitor** accounting folder for new transactions
2. **Categorize** expenses by type and vendor
3. **Track** invoices through draft → sent → paid lifecycle
4. **Analyze** revenue vs goals and spending trends
5. **Flag** unusual expenses and overdue invoices
6. **Generate** monthly profit/loss summaries
7. **Integrate** financial data into CEO Briefing

## Modularity

Extensible with:
- Custom expense categories
- Automated invoice generation from time tracking
- Integration with payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Multi-currency support
- Budget tracking and alerts
