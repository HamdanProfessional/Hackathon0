## Briefing Sections

### Required Sections

1. **Executive Summary** - Brief overview (2-3 sentences)
2. **Revenue Performance** - This week, MTD, trend
3. **Completed Work** - Projects, tasks, social media
4. **Bottlenecks** - Delayed items with root cause
5. **Key Metrics** - Performance vs targets
6. **Proactive Suggestions** - Cost optimization, improvements
7. **Upcoming Deadlines** - This week, next week, this month
8. **Action List** - Prioritized tasks for the week

### Optional Sections

9. **Weekend Alerts** - Urgent emails, calendar updates
10. **Notes** - Additional context or observations
