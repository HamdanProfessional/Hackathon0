# Content Generator - Forms and Templates

## LinkedIn Post Templates

### Industry Insight Post
```markdown
---
type: linkedin_post
platform: LinkedIn
topic: [industry_trend]
created: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SSZ
---

[Hook: Surprising statistic or thought-provoking question]

[Body: 2-3 paragraphs with actionable insights]

[Call to Action: Question to encourage discussion]

#[Hashtags] #[Relevant] #[Topics]
```

### Behind-the-Scenes Post
```markdown
---
type: linkedin_post
platform: LinkedIn
topic: behind_scenes
---

[Hook: Authentic moment or vulnerability]

[Story: Share a lesson learned or challenge faced]

[Takeaway: What others can learn from your experience]

[Question: Ask audience about similar experiences]

#[Industry] #[WorkLife] #[Learning]
```

### Product/Service Announcement
```markdown
---
type: linkedin_post
platform: LinkedIn
topic: product_launch
---

[Hook: Address pain point or opportunity]

[Solution: Introduce product/service]

[Benefits: 3 key outcomes for customers]

[Social Proof: Case study or testimonial]

[Call to Action: Link to learn more]

#[Product] #[Innovation] #[CustomerSuccess]
```

## Email Templates

### Cold Outreach Email
```markdown
---
type: email_draft
purpose: outreach
target: [prospect_role]
---

Subject: [Specific value proposition]

Hi [Name],

[Opening: Personalized observation about their situation]

[Value: What you can help with - 1-2 sentences]

[Credibility: Brief social proof or relevant experience]

[Call to Action: Low-friction next step]

Best regards,
[Your Name]

[P.S. Optional: Additional value or urgency]
```

### Follow-Up Email
```markdown
---
type: email_draft
purpose: follow_up
thread_id: [previous_email_id]
---

Subject: Re: [Previous subject]

Hi [Name],

[Context: Reference previous conversation]

[Value Add: New insight or resource]

[Soft Call to Action: Gentle nudge towards next step]

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## Content Generation Form

**Input Parameters:**
- Platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, Blog, Email)
- Topic/Theme
- Target Audience
- Key Message
- Tone (Professional, Casual, Inspirational, Educational)
- Goals (Engagement, Conversion, Brand Awareness)
- Keywords/Hashtags
- Call to Action

**Output:**
- Platform-optimized content
- Headline variations (3-5 options)
- Body content
- Hashtags/Keywords
- Call to Action
- Character count
- Engagement prediction

```markdown

- [ ] Clear headline/hook

- [ ] On-brand tone and voice

```

## Platform Specifications

### LinkedIn
- **Length:** 1,300-3,000 characters optimal
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Media:** Images, PDFs, documents allowed
- **Best Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am or 5-6pm
- **Engagement:** Questions, polls, mentions

### Twitter/X
- **Length:** 280 characters (premium: 25,000)
- **Hashtags:** 1-2 hashtags
- **Media:** Images, GIFs, videos
- **Best Times:** Weekdays 12-3pm, evenings
- **Engagement:** Threads, quotes, retweets

### Email
- **Subject Line:** 30-50 characters
- **Preview Text:** First 80 characters
- **Body:** 200-500 words
- **CTA:** Single, clear action
- **Personalization:** Use recipient name

## Hashtag Strategy

### By Platform
| Platform | Optimal | Examples |
|----------|---------|----------|
| LinkedIn | 3-5 | #Productivity #RemoteWork #TechTrends |
| Twitter | 1-2 | #AI #Automation #SaaS |
| Instagram | 5-10 | #Business #Entrepreneur #Success |
| Facebook | 2-4 | #Industry #Tips #Community |

### By Purpose
- **Brand**: #[YourBrandName]
- **Niche**: #[Industry] #[Sub-niche]
- **Trending**: #[TrendingTopic]
- **Location**: #[City] #[Region]

## Call-to-Action Library

### Engagement CTAs
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "Share your thoughts below."
- "Tag someone who needs to see this."
- "Save this for later."

### Conversion CTAs
- "Learn more: [link]"
- "Get started today: [link]"
- "Book a free consultation: [link]"
- "Download the guide: [link]"

### Professional CTAs
- "Let's discuss in the comments."
- "DM me to chat about [topic]."
- "Repost if you agree."
- "Follow for more [topic]."
