## Platform Specifications

| Spec | Value |

| Image types | JPG, PNG, PDF |
| Video types | MP4, MOV (max 100MB) |
| Hashtags | 3-5 |
| Best times | Tue-   Thu, 8-10am, 5-6pm |

| Spec | Value |

## Engagement Metrics

### Metrics to Track

**Reach:**
- Impressions
- Unique users reached
- Profile views
- Follower growth rate

**Engagement:**
- Reactions/likes
- Comments
- Shares
- Click-through rate

**Platform Benchmarks:**
| Platform | Good Engagement Rate |
|----------|----------------------|
| LinkedIn | >2% of impressions |
| Instagram | >1% of views (Stories) |
| Twitter | >5% engagement rate |
| Facebook | >0.5% engagement rate |

### Content Performance Indicators

**High Performing:**
- Above platform benchmark for engagement
- High share count
- Comments and discussion
- Save/click actions

**Low Performing:**
- Below platform benchmark
- Low engagement despite high impressions
- Zero engagement after 24 hours

## Content Planning

### Content Mix Strategy

**LinkedIn:**
- 40% Educational (how-to, tips)
- 30% Thought leadership (insights, predictions)
- 20% Personal stories (behind-the-scenes)
- 10% Promotional (updates, offers)

**Twitter/X:**
- 40% Insights and tips
- 30% Curated content sharing
- 20% Original thoughts
- 10% Conversational

**Instagram:**
- 60% Visual content (photos, graphics)
- 30% Stories (daily life, behind-the-scenes)
- 10% Reels (short-form video)
- 10% Carousel posts

**Facebook:**
- 40% Educational content
- 30% Personal stories
- 20% News and trends
- 10% Promotional
